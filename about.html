Get to know us!

Established in 2002, Mother Care Nursing Home is a clinic, nursing home and diagnostic centre providing a range of the latest diagnostic, medical & surgical facilities & equipment and a specialized medical and nursing staff. Mother Care Nursing Home has numbers of beds for intensive care and additional beds for patients who need long-term care.

Dr. Sunita Dhande

A Gynecologist, Obstetrician and Infertility Specialist with an experience of 32 years. She completed MBBS from Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur in 1985 and MD - Obstetrics & Gynaecology from Government Medical College and Hospital, Nagpur in 1989.
