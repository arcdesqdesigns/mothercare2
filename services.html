Medical Services

available at Mother Care Nursing Home.

Although we specialise in providing maternity services , our clinic provides a range of gynaecological services to women of all ages.

Please note that pre-natal determination of sex is punishable according to PNDT Act 1994 Section 23. Sex determination is not practised at Mother Care Nursing Home.

We offer the following services:

  • General Gynaecology,
  • Surgery,
  • Obstetrics,
  • Infertility Clinic,
  • Family Planning,
  • Diagnostics.

  • General Gynaecology

    • Menstrual Problems
    • Infections
    • Premenstrual Syndrome (PMS)
    • Cysts
    • Hormonal Therapy
    • Female Sexual Problems
    • Menopause Counselling and art of living

  • Surgery

    • Dilatation & Curettage
    • Hysterectomy
    • Laparoscopy & Advanced Laparoscopy
    • Ovarian Ablation including Oophorectomy / Ovariectomy
    • Various surgical treatments
    • ICU facility and ICU back−up

  • Obstetrics

    • Comprehensive Child-birth Programme
    • Natural delivery
    • Caesarian Section / C-Section delivery
    • Post−natal Care
    • In-house Paediatricians

  • Infertility Clinic

    • Evaluation of Infertility
    • Pre-conceptional counselling
    • Fertility medications for stimulating ovulation
    • Artificial Insemination / Intrauterine Insemination

  • Family Planning Centre

    • Medical Termination of Pregnancy
